Hello there!

Champaran Marriage Bhavan is a versatile event venue located in the heart of Champaran. It caters to a wide range of celebrations, including weddings, receptions, anniversaries, birthday parties, and corporate events. The venue offers lush gardens, elegant banquet halls, and well-manicured lawns, creating a picturesque setting for memorable occasions. With a commitment to local culture and traditions, Champaran Marriage Bhavan ensures that rituals and customs are respected during ceremonies. Whether it's a romantic wedding, a milestone anniversary, or a corporate gathering, guests can enjoy the scenic surroundings and impeccable hospitality. 🎉🏛️
